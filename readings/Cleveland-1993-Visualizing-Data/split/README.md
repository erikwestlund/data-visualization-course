# Cleveland 1993 Visualizing Data Chapter Chunks

The top-level directory keeps the chapter PDFs. This `split/` directory contains 20-page-or-smaller chunks for LLM-friendly reading.

## Chapters

- `01-introduction/`: 1 chunk, 12 pages total
- `02-univariate-data/`: 4 chunks, 70 pages total
- `03-bivariate-data/`: 5 chunks, 94 pages total
- `04-trivariate-data/`: 5 chunks, 92 pages total
- `05-hypervariate-data/`: 2 chunks, 30 pages total
- `06-multiway-data/`: 2 chunks, 39 pages total

Chunk filenames include chapter, part number, and local chapter page range.
