# Simulated simulated_neighborhood_health_survey_dietary_foods

Source: simulated teaching data generated by `scripts/simulate-teaching-data.R`.

Designed as a swappable cousin for `data/real/nhanes_2021_2023_dietary_foods.csv`.

Topic: neighborhood health survey item-level dietary recall

Unit of observation: one reported food/beverage item

Important caution: these data are fully simulated. They should be used for teaching, practice, and demonstrations only.

Notes:

- Includes Day 1/Day 2 long records, eating occasions, food sources, and selected nutrient components.
