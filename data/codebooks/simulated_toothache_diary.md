# Simulated simulated_toothache_diary

Source: simulated teaching data generated by `scripts/simulate-teaching-data.R`.

Designed as a swappable cousin for `data/real/migraine_headache_diary.csv`.

Topic: toothache diary with care-advice groups

Unit of observation: one person-day

Important caution: these data are fully simulated. They should be used for teaching, practice, and demonstrations only.

Notes:

- Includes unequal diary lengths, negative pre-reference days, treatment/advice groups, and changing toothache probabilities over time.
