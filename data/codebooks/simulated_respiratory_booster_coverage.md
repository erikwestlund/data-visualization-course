# Simulated simulated_respiratory_booster_coverage

Source: simulated teaching data generated by `scripts/simulate-teaching-data.R`.

Designed as a swappable cousin for `data/real/flu_vaccination_age_time_clean.csv`.

Topic: seasonal respiratory booster coverage

Unit of observation: one geography-age-month row

Important caution: these data are fully simulated. They should be used for teaching, practice, and demonstrations only.

Notes:

- Mirrors the flu coverage time-series shape with age, region, month, and season patterns.
