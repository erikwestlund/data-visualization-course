# Simulated simulated_rural_health_indicators

Source: simulated teaching data generated by `scripts/simulate-teaching-data.R`.

Designed as a swappable cousin for `data/real/infant_mortality_countries.csv`.

Topic: rural health indicators by region and income

Unit of observation: one area/country-like unit

Important caution: these data are fully simulated. They should be used for teaching, practice, and demonstrations only.

Notes:

- Includes a nonlinear income-health association, regional clustering, and a small resource-exporter effect.
